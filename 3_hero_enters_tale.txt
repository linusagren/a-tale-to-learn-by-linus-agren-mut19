Our courageous hero gets summoned as a last attempt to save the world that is slowly falling in pieces.
With his outrageous strength and ability to think fast, he will do everything in his power to save the world.
The hero also has his majestic side-kick Bob.
Bob has a special ability that allows him to disguise himself as whatever he pleases, though he generally abuse this ability to hide his scawny body.
Bobs weapon of choice is a magic wand.
